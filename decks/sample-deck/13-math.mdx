# Math Equations

Inline math: Energy is expressed as $E = mc^2$.

Block math (Fourier transform):

$$
f(x) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \hat{f}(\xi) e^{2\pi i \xi x} d\xi
$$

Quadratic formula:

$$
x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}
$$

Euler's identity:

$$
e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0
$$
